El Tumi



**Video is in Spanish so be sure to turn on subtitles!** 

El Tumi is a blade used in ancient times
for ritual and sacrificial purposes by Incan
tibes. There are different tales about what 
this blade represents, some say it represents
the rising of the sun or sun god, others say
the little man on the blade was one of the first
Incans to arrive to the land and thus created that
blade.

3D Tumi Print 1

For my final project I recretaed a replica of El
Tumi in the mincraft server and then printed a 3D
model of approximately 5-6 inches tall. I also printed
a slightly smaller version and left it blank. What I 
found very interesting about choosing this artifact
is being able to see a piece of history through modern 
day technologies.
